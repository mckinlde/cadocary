FAQ

Common questions from WA Defense Attorneys.

  • What does ClientCheck do? ClientCheck reads from JIS and automatically searches for each of your clients, and sends you an email when a new Case is filed.
  • Is this a cloud service? No. ClientCheck is a Windows desktop app.
  • Do you store my court credentials? No. You sign in manually. ClientCheck only automates the tedious clicks once you are logged in.
  • Which courts are supported? ClientCheck is built around https://jislink.courts.wa.gov/ for Washington State.
  • How much does it cost? The ClientCheck license costs $30/month, with a 30 day free trial.
  • Can I cancel any time? Yes. Licenses renew via Stripe and can be canceled at any point.
