What We Do

Cadocary builds one-off software projects to automate tasks that humans hate. Whether you're a public defender sick of creating folders for each client, or a rocket manufacturer trying to comply with state reporting requirements—we can turn your most hated chore into your favourite button.

The Pattern We Solve

Most hated chores follow a familiar pattern:

  • Open a web portal, spreadsheet, or series of documents
  • Do some trivial task based on them
  • Repeat endlessly

The Math is Simple

We only need to know:

  • How long the task takes
  • How often it’s done
  • The exact steps involved

If the annual cost of doing the task outweighs the cost of building a “do-the-task” button, we’ll build it. It’s that simple.

No Budget? No Problem.

It helps if you have a budget in mind, but don’t worry if not—removing mindless toil is almost always worth doing.
