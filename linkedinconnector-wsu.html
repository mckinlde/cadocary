LinkedInConnector.exe

Upload a CSV of LinkedIn profile URLs and let LinkedInConnector.exe send targeted connection requests with safe pacing and a consistent message.

  • Reads profile URLs from a simple CSV.
  • Skips people you’re already connected with.
  • Built-in pacing and random delays to respect LinkedIn limits.
  • Live console so you can watch each invite get sent.

DISCLAIMER: Be careful! LinkedIn will suspend your account for sending too many requests at once. This is primarily to illustrate the types of apps that Cadocary makes, and is distributed for free and without warranty to my classmates as a gift.
